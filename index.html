About Us

Why 900?

Research shows that it takes 15 minutes (900 seconds) of deliberate focus to fully transition your nervous system from a state of scattered anxiety to deep, centered calm. Our app helps you reclaim those perfectly timed segments.

Professional Help

While this tool is designed for daily mental hygiene, it does not replace professional care. If you are struggling, please reach out to dedicated mental health professionals:

  • National Crisis Line: 988
  • Mental Health Resources: www.mentalhealth.gov
  • Therapist Directory: www.psychologytoday.com
